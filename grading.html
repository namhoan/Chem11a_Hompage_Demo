Grading:

Grades will be calculated as follows:
4 Quizzes 20%
2 exams 40%
Final exam 40%
The average grade in Chem. 11a is generally around B-. Letter-grade
equivalences to numerical scores are usually determined based on the
class average and the distribution of scores.
Performing well in the course, however, should not be a competition
with your fellow students. The following table provides guaranteed
letter-grade equivalences for any student whose performance falls
within a specified range.
Any curve can only help, it cannot hurt, your grade. For example, if
the average final score is 62%, that score will be set as the B- and all
other grades will be adjusted around that average. On the other hand,
if the class average is 82%, that does not mean that 82% is now equivalent
to a B-. Rather, it means that the average grade earned in the class would be B+.
